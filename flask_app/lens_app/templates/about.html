About


Hi! My name is Ana. I developed LensExpert to help you find the right lens to snap those perfect photos! The recommendations take into account the type of photos you would like to take, your available budget as well as the popuplarity of each lens according to the discussions at DPreview.com. For each lens, you can see both the original price and the resale price. The resale price is predicted by a model trained on data from the sales posted at FredMiranda.com. These can come in handy if you hope to find the lens in used condition or if you plan to resell the lens, say, after you've had your fill of landscape photos and are ready to move on to another subject. The popularity is relative to the lenses that fall into the specified price range (in terms of original price for the top row, and in terms of expected resale price in the bottom row).

Happy snapping and may pixie dust line all your photos!
